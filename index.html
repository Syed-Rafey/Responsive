Babar Azam

Babar Azam, one of Pakistan's premier cricketers, has quickly risen to prominence as one of the world's finest batsmen. Known for his elegant stroke play and impressive consistency, Babar has become a cornerstone of Pakistan's batting lineup across all formats. Born in Lahore on October 15, 1994, he made his international debut in 2015 and has since set numerous records, including being the fastest Pakistani to 1,000 runs in T20 Internationals. Babar's technique, calm demeanor, and adaptability on the field have drawn comparisons to some of the greatest batsmen of the game. As captain, he has taken on a leadership role, aiming to drive Pakistan to new heights in world cricket. His dedication, both as a player and a leader, reflects his commitment to representing Pakistan on the global stage.
